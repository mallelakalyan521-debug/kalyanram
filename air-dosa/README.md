# 🚁 Air Dosa - Drone Delivery Dosas

Welcome to **Air Dosa**, a futuristic dosa delivery service that brings hot, crispy dosas to your doorstep via autonomous drone delivery!

## 🌟 Features

- **Lightning Fast Delivery**: Get your dosas in just 10-15 minutes
- **Eco-Friendly**: Zero-emission drone delivery reduces carbon footprint
- **Always Hot & Fresh**: Thermally insulated containers keep dosas piping hot
- **Affordable**: No delivery charges! Our drones make delivery cost-effective
- **Easy Ordering**: Simple online ordering through our website
- **Multiple Varieties**: Plain Dosa, Masala Dosa, Paneer Dosa, Cheese Dosa, Onion Dosa, and Egg Dosa

## 🚀 How to Use

1. Open `index.html` in your web browser
2. Browse our menu and select your favorite dosa
3. Add items to your cart
4. Fill in your delivery details (name, email, phone, address)
5. Confirm your order
6. Your drone will deliver your dosas in 10-15 minutes!
